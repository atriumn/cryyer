Hi there,

We’re excited to share what’s new in **Cryyer v0.1.13**. This release is all about giving you more control over how you share your progress.

### More flexibility for your updates
Our biggest addition this week is bringing **multi-audience support** directly into the release pipeline. 

What does this mean for you? Instead of sending the same update to everyone, you now have the foundation to manage and reach different groups of people simultaneously. It’s a huge step toward making your communication more personalized and effective without adding extra work to your plate.

### Why it matters
As your project grows, your audience does too. Whether you're balancing internal stakeholders and external beta testers, or managing different tiers of users, you can now ensure the right people are seeing the right updates at the right time.

Thanks for being part of the journey. We can't wait to hear what you think of these improvements!
