## Cryyer 0.3.0 is here!

We’ve been busy making Cryyer smoother and easier to use. This update focuses on simplifying your workflow and cleaning up our foundations so you can focus on your work.

### What’s New

- **Instant Setup:** Getting started is now faster. We’ve added a way to initialize Cryyer without the back-and-forth prompts, making it much easier to drop into new projects or automate your setup.
- **A New Home on NPM:** We’ve officially moved our package to `@atriumn/cryyer`. This change helps us stay organized and ensures you’re always using the official, most secure version of the tool.
- **Fresher Documentation:** We’ve polished our guides and fixed stale information across the board. Whether you’re just starting out or looking for a specific command, the docs are now more accurate and helpful.
- **Better Reliability:** We fixed several behind-the-scenes bugs and improved how we deliver updates, meaning a more stable experience for you every time you use Cryyer.

Thanks for being part of the journey. We're excited to hear how these improvements help your workflow!
