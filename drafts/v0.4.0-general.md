## Cryyer v0.4.0 is here!

We’ve been hard at work making Cryyer more flexible and easier to get running. This release focuses on giving you more control over your automation.

### Choose your workflow
You can now decide exactly which types of updates you want to generate. With our new pipeline options, you can choose to send weekly digests, release announcements, or both. It’s all about fitting into your existing process.

### Faster, automated initialization
Setting up Cryyer in a new project is now much faster. We’ve added a way to initialize the tool without interactive prompts, which is perfect if you want to script your setup or just get moving quickly.

### Other improvements
- **New Package Name:** We’ve moved to @atriumn/cryyer for better organization.
- **Better Documentation:** We’ve cleaned up our guides to make sure you always have the right information at your fingertips.
- **Reliability Fixes:** We've polished the way emails are built and sent to ensure your updates always reach their destination.

Thanks for being part of the journey!
