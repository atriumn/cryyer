## Cryyer 0.6.0 is here

We’ve been working hard to make Cryyer more flexible and easier to get started with. This update focuses on giving you more control over your data and your workflow.

### Easier Subscriber Management
You can now use a GitHub Gist to store your subscriber list. It’s a simple, low-friction way to manage your audience without setting up a complex database.

### More Control Over Your Updates
We’ve added a new `--pipeline` option that lets you choose exactly what kind of update you want to create. Whether you’re focusing on specific releases or a broader summary, Cryyer now adapts to your needs.

### Faster Onboarding
Setting up a new project is now even quicker. With our new non-interactive initialization, you can get Cryyer ready to go in seconds, whether you're doing it manually or as part of a script.

### Notable Improvements
- **New Package Name:** We’ve moved to `@atriumn/cryyer` on npm to keep things organized.
- **Polished Visuals:** Fixed our status badges so they render correctly on GitHub and look great on your project page.
- **Smarter Drafting:** Improved our automated workflows to ensure your email drafts are created reliably and without duplicates.

Thanks for using Cryyer. We can’t wait to help you send your next update!
