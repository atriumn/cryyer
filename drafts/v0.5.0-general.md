## Cryyer v0.5.0 is here

We've been hard at work making Cryyer more flexible and easier to use. This version focuses on giving you more control over how you share your project's progress.

### What's New

- **Choose your workflow**: You can now easily decide whether you're generating a digest, a release update, or both. It’s all about sending the right message to your audience at the right time.
- **Faster setup**: We’ve introduced a streamlined setup mode that doesn't require manual input. This makes it much quicker to get Cryyer running in your projects, especially if you love automation.
- **Official NPM package**: We've officially moved to `@atriumn/cryyer` to keep our releases organized and easy for you to find.
- **Refreshed documentation**: We've updated our guides to ensure you have the most accurate information as you build and share.

Thanks for being part of the Cryyer journey!
