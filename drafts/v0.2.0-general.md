## Hello from Cryyer!

We’re excited to share that version 0.2.0 is officially here. This update focuses on making the project more reliable and easier to navigate for everyone.

### What’s New

- **A New Home on npm**: We have officially renamed our package to `@atriumn/cryyer`. This makes it easier to find and keeps everything organized under our official namespace.
- **Polished Documentation**: We’ve cleared out old, confusing instructions across the repository. Whether you are a new user or a long-time follower, the guides are now accurate and easy to follow.
- **Reliable Builds**: We’ve fine-tuned our internal processes to ensure that every update we ship is stable and ready for use right out of the gate.

### Why it matters

These changes might seem small, but they lay the groundwork for a much smoother experience. By cleaning up our documentation and standardizing our naming, we’re making it easier for you to build with Cryyer without the guesswork.

Thanks for following along as we grow!
