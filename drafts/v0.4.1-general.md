## Hello from Cryyer

We’ve been busy making Cryyer more flexible and easier to get started with. Here is what is landing in version 0.4.1.

### Better Workflow Control
You now have more power over how your updates are handled. We’ve added new pipeline options that let you choose exactly which types of workflows you want to run—whether you're focusing on release updates, summaries, or both. This gives you total control over your automation.

### Faster Setup
Getting Cryyer running in a new project is now much quicker. We’ve added a non-interactive setup mode, which is perfect for those who want to get configured and moving without clicking through multiple prompts. It’s a smoother, faster way to get your project off the ground.

### Documentation & Refinements
We’ve spent some time cleaning up our documentation to make sure everything is accurate and easy to follow. We also officially moved our package to `@atriumn/cryyer` and polished our release process to ensure everything arrives in your inbox exactly as intended.

- Improved workflow flexibility
- Streamlined project initialization
- Updated documentation for better clarity

Thanks for being part of the journey!
