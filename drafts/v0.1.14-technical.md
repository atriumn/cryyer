## Cryyer v0.1.14

We have released version 0.1.14, focusing on critical fixes for our email rendering engine to ensure consistent output across diverse Markdown inputs.

### Fixes & Improvements

- **Deep Heading and List Item Support**: We addressed an issue where deep headings (H4+) and asterisk-based list items were failing to render correctly in specific email clients. This fix ensures that your templates maintain structural integrity regardless of the markdown flavor used in your source content. (PR #94)
- **Release Lifecycle Management**: Finalized internal automated release chores to improve versioning consistency across the stack. (PR #92)

### Integration Details

This is a non-breaking update. If you are building custom notification flows, you should see improved compatibility with the Cryyer rendering pipeline immediately upon upgrading your dependencies.

View the full release on [GitHub](https://github.com/atriumn/cryyer/releases/tag/v0.1.14).

Thanks for building with Cryyer.
