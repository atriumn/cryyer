## Cryyer 0.7.0 is here!

We’ve been busy making it easier for you to share your updates, not just over email, but across your social channels too. This release focuses on giving you more ways to reach your audience with less effort.

### What’s New

- **Social Media Automation**: You can now generate, draft, and send social posts directly from the command line. We've integrated with Buffer to help you turn your project updates into social content in seconds.
- **GitHub Gist Subscriber Store**: We added a new, simple way to manage your email list using GitHub Gists. It’s a great, lightweight option for keeping your subscriber list in sync without needing a complex database.
- **Simplified Setup**: We've introduced a dedicated configuration directory and included example files to help you get your social sharing and email workflows up and running faster.
- **Better Reliability**: We’ve improved how the system handles content generation to ensure your drafts are created smoothly every time.

### Why it matters

Keeping your community informed shouldn't feel like a chore. With these updates, Cryyer handles the heavy lifting of formatting and distributing your news, so you can spend more time building and less time copy-pasting between apps.

Thanks for following along!
