## Cryyer 0.2.1 is here

This update focuses on making Cryyer more reliable and easier to navigate. Here are the highlights of what’s changed in this version.

### Clearer Documentation
We've gone through the entire project to fix outdated guides. Getting started and finding the information you need should now be a much smoother experience.

### Official Package Rename
To stay organized as we grow, we have renamed our npm package to `@atriumn/cryyer`. This makes it easier for you to find and install our official releases.

### Reliability Improvements
We’ve refined how we handle internal workflows to ensure that drafting and sending emails is more consistent. These fixes address small hiccups in versioning and building from source, making the tool more dependable for your projects.

### Other Improvements
- Fixed version parsing to ensure updates are tracked correctly.
- Cleaned up internal chores to keep the project healthy.
- Improved the reliability of our automated release process.

Thank you for using Cryyer! You can view the full release notes and download the update on [GitHub](https://github.com/atriumn/cryyer/releases).
